About

Expense Tracker is a web-based application that helps individuals and businesses to track and manage their expenses effectively. With this application, users can easily monitor their spending habits, set budgets, and keep track of their financial transactions.

The goal is to provide a simple and user-friendly interface that allows users to manage their expenses effortlessly.Whether you are an individual looking to track your personal expenses or a business owner trying to keep track of your financial transactions, Expense Tracker is the perfect solution for you.
